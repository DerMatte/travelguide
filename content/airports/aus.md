# AUS Airport Guide

> Single-terminal layout and fast security make AUS unusually easy to navigate.

## Quick Facts
- Single terminal with East and West concourses linked by a long walkway.
- Main airlines: Southwest (largest), Delta, United, American.
- No train; expect 8-15 minute walks between farthest gates.
- TSA PreCheck and CLEAR available at the single checkpoint.
- Strong local food post-security; free WiFi throughout.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Main checkpoint opens at 4:30am; lines stay under 20 minutes most days.
- Use PreCheck or CLEAR to skip the longest queues during 6-8am and 4-6pm rushes.
- Families and oversized bags create the only consistent slowdowns.
- No separate international screening; same checkpoint for all flights.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the new Garage B for shortest walk to check-in if flying Southwest.
- Download the AUS app before arrival for live gate changes and security wait times.
- Fill water bottles after security near the yoga room on the upper level.
- Use the quiet seating area past Gate 15 when flights are delayed.
- Grab breakfast tacos at Torchy's instead of waiting for onboard service.
- Walk the moving walkways on the upper level to beat crowds on the lower level.
- Check the airport website for construction alerts before heading to the cell phone lot.

## Terminals & Navigation
- One terminal building; East and West concourses connected airside.
- Moving walkways run the length of the upper level; use them for any gate change over 10 minutes.
- Baggage claim is on the lower level; follow signs to ground transport or parking.
- No need to exit and re-clear for connections.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Centurion Lounge near Gate 14 offers solid food and showers for eligible passengers.
- Salt Lick BBQ and Torchy's Tacos are the standout post-security choices.
- Yoga room and meditation space near the center of the terminal provide rare quiet spots.
- Avoid the overpriced sit-down restaurants near the main atrium.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Garage parking closest and most expensive; use the free shuttle from the economy lot when staying overnight.
- Rideshares pick up on the lower level; expect 10-15 minute waits at peak arrival times.
- Capital Metro bus 20 runs to downtown for $1.25; fastest non-car option.
- Cell phone lot is small and fills quickly during evening banks.
