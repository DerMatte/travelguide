# BER Airport Guide

> Practical, time-saving guide for the connected T1/T2 complex most travelers actually use.

## Quick Facts
- **Terminals:** **T1** (main) and **T2** (select low-cost) are **within walking distance** (~5 min on arrivals level E0). Both share a **common airside area** after security — any checkpoint reaches any gate ([BER terminals](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/orientation/terminals.html)).
- **Rail:** **FEX** airport express to **Hauptbahnhof in ~23 min**, every **15 min** (24/7 except noted timetable changes). **S9** every **20 min**. VBB **ABC ticket €5.00** (2026) covers airport↔city ([BER public transport](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/orientation/getting-here/public-transport.html)).
- **Security:** CT lanes in T1 areas 1 and 5 and all of T2 allow **liquids up to 2 L** and devices in bag; legacy X-ray lanes (T1 areas 2 and 4) still require 100 ml liquids out. **BER Runway slot booking ended 26 May 2026** ([BER security update](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/de/news/2026-05-21-anpassung-siko.html)).
- Free unlimited Wi-Fi works reliably throughout both terminals.
- Luggage storage and **showers (€10, card only)** available landside in T1 ([BER showers](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/cafes-shops-service/sgs-poi/510-duschen-t1.html)).

## Security & Screening Tips
- Arrive at security no later than 2 hours before departure on weekday mornings.
- Check the BER app or terminal monitors and walk to whichever checkpoint shows the lowest wait — **any lane in T1 or T2 reaches any gate**.
- **Lufthansa Group Fast Lane** (Miles & More Frequent Traveller/Senator/HON plus LH Group business/first) uses a dedicated lane — can beat standard CT queues on busy weekdays; check live monitors.
- CT lanes: leave bags packed; legacy lanes still require 100 ml liquids out and laptops out — staff route you; don't assume every queue is CT.
- Liquids and laptops must come out on non-CT lanes — German screeners enforce EU rules strictly there.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Buy a VBB **ABC day ticket** before security; machines are faster landside at the train station (U2).
- If your flight is delayed, exit airside and re-clear; the second security pass is often quicker than waiting airside.
- The quietest seating is on the upper level of the T1 food court, past the escalators.
- Download the official BER app for gate changes — airline apps lag by 10–15 minutes.
- Since CT lanes opened and BER Runway slots ended, follow **live wait times** on the app rather than booking Runway — CT queues are often shorter.
- Regional train platform is one level below arrivals (U2); follow blue "Regionalbahn" signs to skip crowded S-Bahn queues.
- Late-night arrivals (after 11pm) can reach the city on night buses if S-Bahn service is reduced — check VBB journey planner.
- Avoid the central check-in islands; use the outer desks for shorter bag-drop lines.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Terminal 1 is the main building with the train station underneath; walking time from security to farthest gate is 12–15 minutes.
- Terminal 2 is a **~5-minute walk** from T1 via arrivals level E0 — no shuttle bus required ([BER terminals](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/orientation/terminals.html)).
- All lounges, shops, and gates sit airside after security in the shared departure area.
- Moving walkways run only one direction toward the gates; return walks are slower.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- **BER Lounge Tegel (€36)** and **BER Lounge Tempelhof (€48)** are the airport-operated pay lounges — both reachable from T1 or T2 airside ([BER lounges](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/lounges-vip/lounges.html)).
- **Lufthansa Lounge** (Gate B20) is status/cabin only — no day pass sales at BER.
- Landside food hall near the S-Bahn offers better value and seating than airside options.
- For a calm wait, use the seating area above the check-in hall; it stays empty until evening.
- Showers cost **€10** (towel/shampoo included, card only) next to luggage storage on the arrivals level.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **FEX** airport express is fastest to Hauptbahnhof (**~23 min**, **€5.00 ABC** VBB ticket) — runs every **15 min** ([BER public transport](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/orientation/getting-here/public-transport.html)).
- **S9** is the same ABC fare and runs every **20 min** to the city ring.
- Taxi flat rate to central Berlin is €49–55; avoid during rush hour when the train wins.
- Short-term parking P1 charges **€10/hour** on-site ([BER parking](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/parking/preise.html)); **P4 is carsharing-only**, not public long-stay — use P2/P3/P7/P8 or book economy lots online.
