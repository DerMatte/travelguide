# CLT Airport Guide

> Fast, no-fluff guide to moving through Charlotte Douglas with minimal stress and wasted time.

## Quick Facts
- American Airlines dominant hub; most connections are AA metal
- **MCT (AA-published floors — pad for peaks):**
  - **Domestic → domestic:** **45 min** legal minimum; **60–75 min** sane.
  - **International → domestic:** **90 min** floor; immigration can run **20–90 min** — tell staff if under 90 min.
  - **International → international:** **75 min** floor; **90 min+** safer.
- One terminal building; concourses A–E linked by long moving walkways
- Main security checkpoint opens at 4:30 a.m.; PreCheck lane usually shorter
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free airport-wide. **Power:** USB-C/MagSafe tables along A/B walkway near windows.
- Free cell-phone lot directly across from terminal with live flight displays
- Rental cars and rideshare on lower level; no hotel inside the terminal

## Security & Screening Tips
- Arrive at the single main checkpoint; PreCheck and CLEAR are on the far left.
- Peak backup occurs 5:30–8 a.m. and 4–7 p.m.; use the AA app to confirm gate before entering line.
- CLEAR members can bypass the ID check and usually cut 10–15 minutes.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the cell-phone lot and wait for the “landed” text—saves $ on short-term garage.
- If connecting to E, stay on the moving walkway the entire way; tram only saves time with mobility issues.
- Grab coffee and a seat in the Great Hall pre-security; post-security seating is scarce near gates.
- Download the CLT app for real-time tram status and walking times between concourses.
- When running late on an AA connection, use the “CLT Connection” text line for gate agents.
- Rental-car return is fast; follow signs to “Return” and drop at the first available stall.
- Free charging tables with USB-C and MagSafe are along the A/B walkway near the windows.

## Terminals & Navigation
- All flights use the same terminal; only difference is which concourse your gate is on.
- A–B–C–D are walkable in 8–10 minutes; E adds another 5–7 minutes.
- Moving walkways run most of the length; they are reliable except during overnight maintenance.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Club at CLT (paid) in the Great Hall is the only lounge before security and usually quiet.
- Post-security, the best food is at Beatrix or Bad Daddy’s Burger Bar in the E concourse.
- Quietest seating is the window benches overlooking the ramp between B and C.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Daily garage is $24/day; economy lot is $14/day with free shuttle every 10 minutes.
- Rideshare pickup is on the lower arrivals level; follow signs to avoid circling.
- Light-rail (LYNX) Blue Line stops at the airport station; 20-minute ride to Uptown.
