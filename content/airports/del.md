# DEL Airport Guide

> One-page playbook to move through DEL fast — terminal assignment changed when Air India spread across T2 and T3.

## Quick Facts
- **T1:** **IndiGo**, **Akasa**, **SpiceJet**, and **Air India Express** domestic.
- **T2:** **60 Air India domestic departures per day** (from **26 Oct 2025**) — flight numbers starting with **1** indicate T2; verify on your ticket.
- **T3:** **All international** flights plus **~120 Air India domestic** departures daily; **Air India** long-haul hub.
- **Vistara:** Merged into **Air India** on **12 Nov 2024** — no separate Vistara terminal or check-in; former Vistara flights use **AI** flight numbers (often four digits starting with **2**).
- **Inter-terminal transfer:** Free shuttle every **20 min** between **T1, T2, T3**; **T2 ↔ T3** also connected by a **covered landside walkway** at arrivals. Budget **30–45 min** including wait.
- **No airside transfer** between terminals — changing terminals means landside movement and new security.
- **Peak chaos:** **05:00–09:00** and **18:00–23:00**; arrive **3 h** early international, **2.5 h** domestic.

## Security & Screening Tips
- **T3 international:** Chronically slow at peaks — paid **fast-track** on departures level (roughly **₹1,200–1,800**) when queues exceed **30 min**.
- **Liquids:** Strictly enforced — buy after security or carry an empty bottle for fountains.
- **T1 domestic:** Faster than T3 but still allow **25–35 min** for security.
- **T2:** Smaller checkpoint — can be quicker for eligible Air India domestic flights.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Check terminal by flight number:** Air India domestic **T2** flights use numbers starting with **1** — don't assume all Air India is in **T3**.
- **T3 Gate 3 (far left):** Often shorter check-in queues for **Air India** and **Star Alliance** during peaks.
- **Inter-terminal shuttle:** Only use if you have **45+ min** between terminals; prepaid taxi **₹150–200** is faster in a pinch.
- **Plaza Premium (T3, near gate 33):** Best value day-pass lounge — showers and quiet zones.
- **Metro:** **Airport Express Line** from **T3** — fastest exit to central Delhi; trains roughly **04:45–23:30**.
- **Avoid:** Assuming **Vistara** still operates separately — it's **Air India** now.

## Terminals & Navigation
- **T1 ↔ T2 ↔ T3:** Not walkable as a through-airside route; shuttle or **T2–T3 walkway** (landside) only.
- **T3:** Modern and well-signed; longest walks to far gates — allow **15 min** gate-to-gate.
- **T1:** Older and more chaotic — factor extra walking time.
- **International → domestic connection:** Even with through-checked bags, you **collect bags at arrivals**, clear customs, and re-check at the transfer desk before proceeding to **T2** or **T1** — budget **2.5–3 h**.

## Lounges Food & Amenities
- **Plaza Premium T3 (near gate 33):** Showers, decent food, quiet zones — best paid option.
- **Food:** **Haldiram's** and **Sagar Ratna** in T3 departures; avoid overpriced international chains.
- **Quiet spot:** Seating past **gate 50** at the end of the T3 pier.
- **T1:** Almost no quality paid lounge — eat before arrival for LCC domestic.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **Delhi Metro Airport Express (T3):** Fastest and cheapest to **New Delhi** station — roughly **₹60–90**, **20–25 min**.
- **App cabs (Uber/Ola):** Designated pickup zones — **10–15 min** walk from arrivals.
- **Prepaid taxis:** Reliable but **30–50%** more than apps.
- **Parking:** T3 short-stay **₹120/hr**; long-term cheaper with shuttle.
