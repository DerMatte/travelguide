# DEN Airport Guide

> Practical, time-saving guide to moving through Denver International Airport without delays or wasted effort.

## Quick Facts
- **Layout:** One **Jeppesen Terminal** feeds **Concourses A, B, C** via underground automated train (**~5–8 min** ride).
- **United** dominates **Concourse B**; **Frontier** and **Southwest** split **A** and **C**.
- **Security (May 2026):** All screening on **Level 6** — **East** and **West** checkpoints only; old **South Security (Level 5) closed 5 Aug 2025** ([TSA](https://www.tsa.gov/news/press/releases/2025/08/04/tsa-begin-operations-new-east-security-checkpoint-den)).
- **CT scanners:** Both checkpoints use computed tomography — **laptops and compliant liquids can stay in your bag** at most lanes (still subject to TSA liquid limits).
- **Between concourses:** No security re-screening — stay airside on the train.
- **Long gate walks:** Moving walkways help, but **B ↔ C** via train still budget **15–25 min** gate-to-gate.

## Security & Screening Tips
- **Pick your checkpoint:** **West** (near United) if departing **Concourse B**; **East** (near Southwest) if departing **Concourse C** — both lead to the same train platform on **Level 4**.
- **Hours:** **03:00–01:00** daily at both checkpoints.
- **Peak windows:** **05:00–08:00** and **16:00–19:00** — add **15–25 min** buffer; snow days add more.
- **CLEAR + PreCheck:** Available at **both** Level 6 checkpoints.
- **DEN Reserve:** Time-slot screening available at select checkpoints — check flydenver.com day of travel.
- **Arrive:** **90 min** domestic, **2.5 h** international; add **30 min** in winter storms.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Level 6 only:** If you're coming from the **A Line**, go **upstairs to Level 6** for security — Level 5 checkpoints are closed.
- **Train tip:** Board at the far end during peaks for a seat; **B ↔ C** direct train is usually fastest.
- **Concourse A bridge:** Walk from **Level 6** via the **A-Bridge** to **Concourse A** without taking the train — useful for **A** gates only.
- **Meditation room (Concourse A, near A34):** Quiet with outlets — rarely crowded.
- **Download the flydenver app** for train and security wait times.

## Terminals & Navigation
- **All gates require the train** (except **A-Bridge** to Concourse A).
- **Walking B ↔ C without train:** **25+ min** and impractical — use the train.
- **Baggage claim:** Level **5** — allow **15 min** from train arrival.
- **Recommended connection buffers:**
  - Same concourse: **45–60 min**
  - Concourse change (airside): **60–75 min**
  - Separate tickets: **2.5+ h**

## Lounges Food & Amenities
- **United Club (Concourse B):** Largest club — less crowded after **20:00**.
- **Great Hall (Level 5/6 pre-train):** Better food variety than many gate areas — eat before boarding the train if you have time.
- **DEN Market:** Solid grab-and-go sandwiches and local beer in the terminal.
- **Power:** Upper-level window seating in Jeppesen Terminal — charge before the train ride.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **RTD A Line:** **$11.25** to **Union Station** — **~37 min**; runs every **15–30 min** (can delay in heavy snow).
- **Rideshare:** Pickup on **Level 5** — **10–20 min** wait during peak arrivals.
- **Economy parking:** **~$15/day** with free shuttle; garage **~$32/day** but closer.
- **Snow:** A Line most vulnerable to weather — have rideshare backup in winter.
