# DTW Airport Guide

> Fast, no-fluff guide to moving through Detroit’s two-terminal hub with minimal stress.

## Quick Facts
- Delta operates the McNamara Terminal; all other airlines use the North Terminal.
- McNamara features moving walkways and an indoor tram serving A1–A78 gates.
- 8–12 minute covered walkway connects McNamara to North Terminal.
- Both terminals offer Clear and TSA PreCheck lanes.
- Cell-phone lots are free and well-marked on both sides of the airport.
- Winter weather can add 20–30 minutes to curb-to-gate time.

## Security & Screening Tips
- McNamara PreCheck lane is consistently faster than North Terminal.
- Clear enrollment kiosks sit just inside McNamara; use them if your flight is within 90 minutes.
- Avoid North Terminal security 6–8 a.m. and 4–7 p.m.; lines frequently exceed 30 minutes.
- Families and oversized bags are routed to standard lanes at both terminals.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the DTW app the night before; it pushes terminal changes faster than airline apps.
- Park in the North Terminal garage if flying non-Delta—shorter walk and usually cheaper.
- Use the McNamara A38–A57 walkway shortcut when connecting from international arrivals.
- Grab coffee at the Starbucks before security in McNamara; post-security lines are longer.
- If your connection is tight, text “DTW” to your airline for gate info instead of hunting screens.
- The outdoor smoking area near North Terminal baggage claim is the only legal spot airside.
- PreCheck + Clear combo at McNamara often gets you through in under 10 minutes even at peak.

## Terminals & Navigation
- McNamara: A concourses connected by tram; allow 12 minutes between farthest gates.
- North Terminal: Single-level layout; all gates reachable in 8 minutes or less.
- Inter-terminal walkway is fully indoors and signposted; follow purple “Terminal to Terminal” signs.
- Common mistake: assuming all Delta flights depart McNamara—double-check boarding pass.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- Delta Sky Club in McNamara A38 offers solid breakfast and showers; day passes available.
- Detroit Beer Co. in McNamara post-security has local drafts and quick table service.
- Shake Shack in McNamara A concourse is reliable and faster than sit-down spots.
- Quietest seating is the A57–A78 end of McNamara; fewer announcements and charging stations.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- SMART bus #125 runs to downtown Detroit every 30–45 minutes for $2.
- Taxi and rideshare stands are on the lower level of both terminals; Uber/Lyft pickup is clearly signed.
- McNamara garage is $3/hour, $24/day; North Terminal garage is $2/hour, $18/day.
- Cell-phone lots fill quickly on snow days—have a backup plan or circle the perimeter road.
