# DXB Airport Guide

> World's busiest international hub — smooth for Emirates-to-Emirates in T3, unforgiving when you change terminals or hold separate tickets.

## Quick Facts
- **Terminals:** **T3** (Emirates + Qantas exclusively; concourses **A**, **B**, **C**), **T1** (most other international carriers; **D gates** via airside train), **T2** (flydubai, regional/charter, **F gates**).
- **MCT (same ticket, practical floor):**
  - **T3 → T3** (Emirates connection, bags through): **90 min** floor, **2 h** comfortable — concourse changes add time.
  - **T3 concourse change (B/C → A):** Add **15–25 min** for internal metro/bus.
  - **T1 ↔ T3** (one ticket, airside where permitted): **2–2.5 h** — inter-terminal transfer dominates.
  - **Separate tickets:** Immigration, baggage, re-check — **3–5 h** minimum for any terminal change ([DXB transfers](https://www.dubaiairports.ae/information/transfers)).
  - **Peak buffer:** Evening Emirates bank (**20:00–23:00**) — add **15–20 min** to any MCT above.
- **No universal airside link T1↔T3:** Free **shuttle buses** every **~30 min**, **24/7**; journey **10–20 min** on bus plus wait/walk = **30–45 min** end-to-end ([Marhaba connections guide](https://www.marhabaservices.com/ae/english/guides/tips-for-dubai-international-airport-connections.html)).
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free airport Wi-Fi — registration required; can slow during peak banks.
- **Liquids:** **100 ml** rule strictly enforced; expect thorough screening.

## Security & Screening Tips
- **Peak pain:** **06:00–09:00** and **20:00–23:00** when Emirates long-haul banks overlap — add **20–30 min** buffer.
- **Connection security:** Required on many transfers — especially **T1 ↔ T3** and certain origin/destination combinations. Follow **TRANSFER** signs; don't drift into arrivals immigration unless entering UAE.
- **Emirates Business/First:** Dedicated lanes in T3 — use if eligible.
- **Liquids/electronics:** Laptops out unless directed otherwise; power banks in carry-on only.
- **Separate tickets:** Must clear **passport control**, collect bags, check in again — no airside shortcut ([DXB transfers](https://www.dubaiairports.ae/information/transfers)).

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Emirates connection flow:** Follow **Connections** signs immediately after landing — bags transfer automatically on one ticket; still check **both** arrival and departure concourse on boarding pass.
- **Concourse A trap:** **A gates** (A380) require the **internal airport train** from B/C — budget **20–30 min** gate-to-gate even without security.
- **T3 internal movement:** Walk between **B ↔ C**; train/bus to **A** and between **A ↔ C** (Bus Connection A6 or connections desk E in B).
- **Inter-terminal bus:** Free, **~30 min** frequency — don't cut a **2 h** connection involving T1↔T3; experienced travelers want **3 h+**.
- **Metro (landside T1↔T3):** Red Line stations at T1 and T3 — useful if you've exited airside; **does not serve T2**.
- **Free showers (T3 Concourse B):** Rarely crowded before **07:00** — bring towel or buy at duty-free.
- **Nol card:** Buy **Nol Silver** at airport Metro station for seamless city travel — works on buses too.
- **Power:** Quiet charging near gates **B30–B40** in T3 vs. crowded central areas.
- **Avoid:** Assuming T1 and T3 are "connected" like a single terminal; booking **90 min** self-transfer T3→T1; using unofficial taxi touts at arrivals.

## Terminals & Navigation
- **T3 scale:** One of the world's largest terminals — farthest B to A gates can exceed **30 min** with train waits.
- **T1 D gates:** Short **airside train** ride from main T1 hall.
- **T2 isolation:** Physically separate — shuttle from T1 (**~20–30 min**) or T3 (**~30–40 min**).
- **Transfer times (practical):**
  - Within T3, same concourse: **10–15 min**
  - T3 B/C → A: **20–30 min**
  - T3 ↔ T1 (bus, end-to-end): **30–45 min**
  - T2 ↔ T1 or T3: **30–45 min**
- **Recommended buffers:**
  - Emirates T3 → T3: **90 min** floor, **2 h** comfortable
  - Any terminal change: **2.5–3 h** one ticket, **4–5 h** separate tickets
- **DXB ↔ DWC (Al Maktoum):** Different airport **~60 km** — taxi or Metro to Ibn Battuta + bus; **not** a quick connection.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- **Emirates lounges (T3):** Business Class lounges in B and C; **First Class** in A — outstanding but status/cabin only. **Overcrowded** during evening departure bank.
- **Marhaba (T1/T3):** Best **paid/PP** play — showers, buffet, reliable Wi-Fi. **T3** location beats T1 for most connections.
- **Food — actually worth it:** **T3 Concourse B** area near **B30** for sit-down options; **T1** is weaker and more expensive. T2 food courts are last resort.
- **Overrated:** Generic duty-free dining at peak prices; trekking to T3 food from T1 on a tight connection.
- **Water:** Refill stations exist airside in T1/T3 but coverage is patchy — bring an empty bottle; bottled water is airport-priced when you cannot find a fountain.
- **Wi‑Fi detail:** Network is free but registration-heavy; sessions drop during evening Emirates banks — re-login if your gate changes mid-wait.
- **Long layover:** **Dubai International Hotel** airside in T3; **sleep pods** in various concourses — verify current pricing.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **Best value to city:** **Dubai Metro Red Line** from T1 or T3 — **AED 5–15**, **45–60 min** to downtown/Burj Khalifa area; runs frequently until late (check RTA for last train).
- **Taxi:** Metered, official rank only — **AED 50–70** to Downtown from T1/T3; higher from T2.
- **T2 access:** Metro does **not** serve T2 — taxi or shuttle only.
- **RTA buses:** **24/7** network; Nol card required.
- **Parking:** T3 long-stay best rates for multi-day; short-stay punishing for pick-ups.
- **Rush hour:** Sheikh Zayed Road clogs **07:00–09:30** and **17:00–20:00** — Metro often beats taxi to Downtown.
