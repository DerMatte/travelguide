# GLA Airport Guide

> One compact reference for the single-terminal airport that saves time on every trip through Glasgow.

## Quick Facts
- **Single terminal** — all domestic and international flights under one roof.
- **MCT (same ticket):** **45 min** floor for same-zone connections; **60 min** if changing passport areas — walks are short but morning peaks kill queues.
- **Carriers:** easyJet, British Airways, Ryanair, Loganair.
- **Security hours:** **04:00–21:00**; last international departures usually **~22:30**.
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free with captive portal — adequate for gate alerts.
- **Water:** Refill points landside near departures mezzanine; airside fountains limited and close with shops after **21:00**.
- **Power:** Upper-level seating between gates **C–D** has the best outlet density.
- **No rail at airport** — **First Bus 500** or taxi to city; Paisley Gilmour Street via bus link.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Liquids in 100 ml containers only; no fast-track lane exists.
- Peak queues hit 45-60 minutes on weekday mornings and Friday evenings.
- Families and passengers with Clear-style assistance can use the priority lane on request.
- Remove laptops and liquids at the belt; staff are strict on aerosols.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the short-stay multi-storey and walk directly to departures in 3 minutes.
- Download the Glasgow Airport app for live gate pushes 15 minutes ahead of screens.
- If your flight is delayed, exit security and return via the mezzanine café for better seating.
- Use the free bus to Paisley Gilmour Street when trains to Glasgow are faster than taxis in rush hour.
- Pre-book the long-stay car park online; drive-up rates are double.
- For early flights, the 24-hour Premier Inn across the road lets you check bags at 04:00.
- Stand at the far end of the baggage hall for quickest access to the taxi rank.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Single terminal; arrivals and departures on the same level.
- Security to farthest gate (B44) takes 7-8 minutes on foot.
- A and B gates are split by a short corridor; follow overhead signs rather than staff directions.
- No moving walkways; budget extra time with heavy bags.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Escape Lounge is small and often crowded; skip it unless you have a long delay.
- Best coffee and seating is the mezzanine café landside before security.
- Airside food is limited to a Wetherspoons and a sandwich bar that close by 21:00.
- Quietest seating is the row of chairs opposite gate A1.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Taxi to city centre costs £25-30 and takes 25-40 minutes depending on traffic.
- First Bus 500 runs every 10-15 minutes to Buchanan Bus Station (£8.50).
- Pre-book long-stay parking at £4.50 per day; short-stay is £4.50 per hour.
- Drop-off is free for 10 minutes in the designated lane; longer stays are charged.
