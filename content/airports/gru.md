# GRU Airport Guide

> Use this page to cut wait times and avoid the most common GRU pain points.

## Quick Facts
- Three terminals; T3 is the international hub for LATAM and most foreign carriers.
- Free 30-minute Wi-Fi; buy 24-hour passes at kiosks for R$25.
- Immigration and customs are single-file bottlenecks on evening arrivals.
- T1 domestic only; T2 mixed; T3 international departures and arrivals.
- Covered walkway links T2 and T3; free shuttle runs every 10 minutes otherwise.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Remove liquids and electronics before the X-ray; agents are strict.
- PreCheck-equivalent (Brazilian “Via Rápida”) is rarely faster than regular lanes.
- Avoid 18:00–22:00 if possible; lines regularly exceed 40 minutes.
- Families and elderly get priority lanes—use them if eligible.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Clear immigration first, then re-check bags for domestic connections; saves 30–40 min versus airline transfer desks.
- Buy a prepaid SIM at T3 arrivals (TIM or Vivo) before the taxi queue; signal is weak inside the terminal.
- Use the upper-level departures curb for drop-off; arrivals curb is always jammed.
- Download offline maps of T3; signage is poor after passport control.
- Stand in the “Brasileiros” immigration line if you hold a Brazilian passport or residency—often half the length.
- For early flights, sleep in the T3 landside food court; airside benches are removed nightly.
- Carry small bills for the R$5–10 shuttle tip; drivers ignore cards.

## Terminals & Navigation
- T3 departures: follow purple signs from check-in; security is one floor up.
- T2–T3 walkway takes 8–12 minutes; shuttle takes 10–15 minutes including wait.
- Minimum connection time posted at 1 h 30 min; allow 2 h 15 min on LATAM codeshares.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- Star Alliance Lounge in T3 airside is the only reliably quiet paid option (day pass ~US$60).
- Landside T3 food court has better Brazilian options (Virado à Paulista, pastelaria) than airside.
- No true quiet zones; best bet is the far end of T3 gates 300–330 after 23:00.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Official taxi desk (Coopertax) at arrivals; fixed rates to Paulista ~R$140.
- Uber pickup is one level above arrivals; follow signs to “Embarque de Aplicativos.”
- Long-term parking (Estacionamento Remoto) is R$35/day with free shuttle every 20 min.
