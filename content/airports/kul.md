# KUL Airport Guide

> Fast-moving hub with two distant terminals; plan transfers and immigration timing carefully to avoid delays.

## Quick Facts
- **Terminals:** **KLIA** (mainline/Malaysia Airlines) and **KLIA2** (AirAsia/LCC) — **~2 km** apart, treated as separate for immigration ([klia2.com transit guide](https://klia2.com.my/passenger_guides/transit_between_KLIA2_and_KLIA.htm)).
- **MCT (same ticket, practical floor):**
  - **Same terminal, int'l → int'l:** **60–75 min** floor, **90 min** comfortable.
  - **KLIA ↔ KLIA2 (one ticket, bags through):** **90 min** airline min, **2 h** practical — Ekspres train **3 min** ride plus immigration/security variables.
  - **Separate tickets:** **3–4 h** minimum — must clear immigration, collect bags, re-check.
  - **Airside coach link:** Phased rollout expected **2026** — verify day-of; do not assume it exists yet.
- **Rail:** **KLIA Ekspres** to KL Sentral **28 min** (**MYR 55**).
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free but unreliable — buy a **7-Eleven SIM** at arrivals for Grab and gate alerts.
- **Water:** Refill points in Satellite Building near gates **C1–C10**; KLIA2 airside fountains sparse.
- **Power:** Under-seat sockets in Satellite Building **C1–C10** — rarely camped.
- **Peaks:** Immigration **14:00–20:00** arrival waves — pad **45+ min**.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Remove liquids and laptops at every lane; no PreCheck equivalent exists.
- Use the KLIA2 premium lane (MYR 50) only if queues exceed 30 people.
- Avoid security between 6-9am and 6-9pm when domestic and regional flights surge.
- Liquids in clear bags are strictly enforced; large toiletries are confiscated without exception.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the MyAirport app before arrival for live gate info and shuttle schedules.
- Clear immigration at KLIA Satellite Building when possible; it is often faster than the main hall.
- Buy KLIA Ekspres tickets online the day before for a small discount and skip the queue.
- If connecting KLIA ↔ KLIA2 on **one ticket** with through-checked bags, use the **KLIA Ekspres** inter-terminal train (**3 min**) or landside shuttle — still allow **2 h** minimum; immigration can bite even on protected connections.
- **Do not assume airside transfer** between KLIA and KLIA2 as of mid-2026 — most itineraries require landside immigration unless your airline confirms an airside coach (phased rollout in progress).
- Charge devices at the power sockets under the seating rows in Satellite Building; they are rarely used.
- Grab a cheap SIM at the arrival hall 7-Eleven before the taxi queue; airport Wi-Fi is unreliable.
- For early flights, sleep in the Satellite Building quiet zone near gate C8; it stays cooler and darker.
- Avoid the main KLIA2 food court after 10pm; most stalls close and prices rise at the 24-hour options.

## Terminals & Navigation
- KLIA and KLIA2 sit **2 km** apart; **KLIA Ekspres** (**3 min**) or free landside shuttle (**10–15 min**) between them.
- Walking between terminals requires exiting and re-entering security — never attempt on a tight connection.
- Satellite Building gates (**A, B, C**) reached by train from KLIA Main — allow **15 min**.
- KLIA2 gates spread across two levels — check the app for the correct pier before leaving check-in.
- **Recommended buffers:**
  - Same terminal: **75 min** floor
  - KLIA ↔ KLIA2, one ticket: **2 h** floor
  - Separate tickets: **3–4 h**

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The best paid lounge is Plaza Premium at KLIA Satellite; it offers solid showers and quiet seating.
- Airside at KLIA2, the Golden Lounge near gate P8 has better food than most paid options.
- For local food, the Satellite Building nasi lemak and roti stalls beat KLIA2 chains on taste and price.
- Quiet seating with charging is easiest in the Satellite Building near gates C1-C10.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- KLIA Ekspres is fastest to KL Sentral (MYR 55); book ahead during evening rush.
- Grab or airport taxis use fixed rates; insist on the coupon system to avoid haggling.
- Long-term parking is cheaper at the KLIA2 open lot; the covered garage fills by 8am.
- Ride-hailing pickup is on Level 1 arrivals; allow 10 extra minutes during rain.
