# NCE Airport Guide

> Practical, no-fluff guide to moving through Nice Côte d'Azur Airport quickly and with minimal stress.

## Quick Facts
- Two terminals linked by free shuttle every 5-10 minutes.
- Main airlines: EasyJet (T1), Air France (T2), British Airways, Lufthansa.
- Security is French standard; liquids 100 ml rule strictly enforced.
- Tram line 2 from Terminal 1 to Nice center in ~20 minutes.
- Currency: euros; ATMs available in both terminals but limited in T1.
- Free Wi-Fi works well throughout; no login required.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Arrive at least 2 hours early for T1 departures; lines often exceed 30 minutes during morning and evening peaks.
- Remove laptops and liquids in advance; bins are small and slow.
- Families and passengers with reduced mobility can use the dedicated “Famille” lane in both terminals.
- No Clear or PreCheck equivalent; the French “Sûreté” registered traveler program is rarely worth the hassle for one-off trips.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the official Nice Airport app before arrival for real-time gate and security wait data.
- If your flight is in T2 after 8 pm, eat in the city and return via tram; food options shrink dramatically.
- Taxi rank at T1 is faster than T2 at peak times; walk the 8-minute shuttle if your bag is light.
- Keep your boarding pass on your phone; paper copies are rarely requested after security.
- Quietest seating is the far end of T2 near gates A60-A70; power outlets are plentiful there.
- Buy a 24-hour tram/bus pass (€5) at the machine outside arrivals instead of single tickets if you plan multiple city trips.
- Avoid the rental-car desks inside the terminal; rates are 15-20 % higher than booking online in advance.

## Terminals & Navigation
- T1 handles most low-cost and non-Schengen flights; T2 is mainly Air France and Schengen.
- Free shuttle runs continuously between terminals; allow 10 minutes including wait.
- Walking between T1 and T2 is possible but takes 15-20 minutes with luggage.
- Both terminals have clear signage in French and English; follow yellow “Départs” signs after security.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- Le Balthazar (T2) offers the best sit-down meal with local specialties.
- Paul and Exki provide reliable quick bites in T1.
- The only paid lounge is the Nice Côte d'Azur Lounge in T2; access via Priority Pass or day pass (€45).
- Charging stations and quiet zones are concentrated near gates A50-A70 in T2.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Tram line 2 from Terminal 1 is cheapest and fastest to city center (€1.50, 20 min).
- Taxis to Nice center cost €30-40; fixed rate to Cannes or Monaco available at rank.
- Short-term parking P1/P2 is €3.50 per hour; long-term P6 is €12 per day but requires shuttle.
- Book valet or off-site parking in advance during July-August high season.
