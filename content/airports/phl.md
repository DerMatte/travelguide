# PHL Airport Guide

> Fast, no-fluff guide to moving through Philadelphia International with minimal stress and wasted time.

## Quick Facts
- American Airlines operates the vast majority of flights from Terminals A and B.
- **MCT (same ticket, airside A–F):** **45 min** floor — **60 min** safer; international arrivals must re-clear in Terminal A.
- All terminals are connected after security; no need to exit and re-clear for domestic connections.
- TSA PreCheck lanes exist at A-West, B, and C checkpoints.
- CLEAR is available at A-West and B; pairing it with PreCheck is fastest.
- SEPTA Regional Rail Airport Line runs every 30 minutes to 30th Street Station.
- Short-term parking garages sit directly above terminals A, B, and C.
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free **PHL-WiFi** — browser login; reliable.
- **Power:** Outlets at D14–D16 and Centurion Lounge area — charge early during AA banks.
- **Water:** Refill fountains post-security in Terminals D and E.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Check the official PHL app or tsa.gov for live wait times before leaving the car.
- A-West and B checkpoints are usually fastest for PreCheck/CLEAR.
- Avoid 6–9 a.m. and 4–7 p.m. if possible; those windows routinely exceed 30 minutes.
- International arrivals must clear security again in Terminal A after customs.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the Terminal B garage even if flying AA out of A—airside walk is under 8 minutes.
- Use the moving walkways between B/C and D/E/F to cut connection time in half.
- Grab coffee at La Colombe in D before heading to your gate; outlets and seating are plentiful.
- If you have a long layover, shower at the Centurion Lounge then work in the quiet zone.
- Download the SEPTA app and buy a one-way ticket on your phone to skip the kiosk line.
- When all A checkpoints are jammed, walk airside to B or C—often 10–15 minutes faster.
- Terminal F has the shortest security lines after 8 p.m. for late departures.

## Terminals & Navigation
- A-West handles most international and wide-body flights; A-East is domestic.
- B/C and D/E/F are linked by long corridors with moving walkways.
- Walking time from A to F security is roughly 12–15 minutes at normal pace.
- Never exit to the landside shuttle unless you have to change terminals before security.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Centurion Lounge in A is the clear winner for food, showers, and quiet space.
- AA Admirals Clubs are functional but crowded; skip the B location unless you need a shower immediately.
- Terminal D has the best mix of sit-down food and seating; Terminal A post-security is cramped.
- Quietest seating is usually near gates D14–D16 or the Centurion Lounge.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- SEPTA Airport Line is cheapest and fastest to Center City ($6.50, 25 minutes).
- Uber/Lyft pickup is on the lower level; follow signs to avoid the taxi stand scrum.
- Short-term garage rates are $4 per half-hour; long-term lots are cheaper but require a shuttle.
- Avoid the cell-phone lot during evening rush—it fills quickly.
