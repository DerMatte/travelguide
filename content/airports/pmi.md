# PMI Airport Guide

> Single-terminal airport where timing and module awareness prevent the biggest summer delays.

## Quick Facts
- Single terminal with three modules (A, B, C) connected by long corridors.
- Dominant carriers: Ryanair (Module A), easyJet and Vueling (Module B).
- 8 km from Palma; EMT bus 1 runs every 15 min, €5; taxi flat rate €25–30.
- No train or people-mover between modules; all transfers are walking.
- Peak season (Jun–Sep) sees frequent 45–60 min security waits.
- Free Wi-Fi is reliable but requires captcha every 45 min.
- **Power:** Window area near Gate B10 has outlets and sea views — best free charging spot.
- **Water:** OpenCor supermarket landside for bottles; limited airside refill — fill before security in summer.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Arrive at least 2.5 hours before departure May–September; 2 hours suffices off-season.
- Use the AENA app for live queue times; the shortest line is usually Module C.
- Liquids in 100 ml containers only; no PreCheck equivalent.
- Families and passengers with reduced mobility get a dedicated lane at Module B.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the AENA app and enable push notifications for gate changes before entering security.
- If your flight is in Module A, check in online and go straight to the far-left security lane—it moves fastest.
- Buy a coffee and ensaimada in the public departures hall; airside food is limited to two cafés with long lines.
- For early flights, the EMT night bus A32 runs until 01:30 and costs €5.
- Park in the P1 express lot only if your return is after 22:00; cheaper long-stay P4 is a 7-minute free shuttle.
- Keep your boarding pass visible—staff at the module entrances still perform random document checks.
- If connecting to a cruise, the cruise terminal bus leaves from the arrivals level curb every 30 min (€8).

## Terminals & Navigation
- All check-in, security, and gates sit within the same building; Modules A–C are 8–12 min walks apart.
- Follow overhead signs for your airline’s module; once airside you cannot return to change modules.
- Baggage claim is on the ground floor directly below each module—allow 15 min after landing.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The only paid lounge (Sala VIP) is airside in Module B; €35 day pass includes basic food and showers.
- Best free seating is the window area near Gate B10 with power outlets and sea views.
- Public departures level has a decent supermarket (OpenCor) for water and snacks before security.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Taxi rank is outside arrivals; insist on the official flat rate to Palma.
- Bus 1 to Plaça d’Espanya is fastest and cheapest; runs 05:00–23:00.
- Long-stay parking P4 costs €8.50/day; book online for 10 % discount and guaranteed shuttle.
