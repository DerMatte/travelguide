# RDU Airport Guide

> Two compact terminals and predictable security make RDU one of the easiest major airports for quick turns.

## Quick Facts
- Terminals 1 and 2 linked by a 5-minute indoor walkway with moving walkways.
- Delta and American dominate Terminal 2; United and Southwest use Terminal 1.
- TSA PreCheck lanes at both terminals; CLEAR only at Terminal 2.
- Free cell phone lot sits 2 minutes from both terminals with live flight displays.
- No airside connection between terminals—re-clear security if you switch.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the RDU app or website for live wait times before leaving the cell phone lot.
- PreCheck lines stay under 10 minutes even during peaks; standard lines hit 30+ minutes 6:30-8:30am and 4:30-7pm.
- CLEAR is only at Terminal 2 and rarely saves time over PreCheck.
- Families and oversized bags slow the standard lanes—use the far-left lane in Terminal 2 when available.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the cell phone lot and wait there until your passenger texts from baggage claim—saves $ on hourly garage fees.
- Terminal 2 security is usually faster after 8pm even on busy days.
- The walkway between terminals has clean restrooms and power outlets that are rarely crowded.
- If your flight is in Terminal 1, check in at Terminal 2 first for shorter bag-drop lines when American or Delta are slow.
- Download the RDU app for gate-change alerts that beat airline push notifications.
- The short-term garage (closest to Terminal 2) has the fastest exit during evening rush.
- Southwest flyers: use Terminal 1 security then walk to Terminal 2 if the T1 line is backed up.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Walkway between T1 and T2 is climate-controlled and clearly signed; no need for the outdoor shuttle.
- Terminal 2 is larger and has more gates—allow 8 minutes to walk from security to farthest gates.
- Common mistake: following signs for “Terminal 1 Departures” when already airside in Terminal 2.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Club at RDU (Terminal 2) is the only paid lounge; day passes available but often full.
- LaFarm Bakery in Terminal 2 post-security has reliable seating and solid coffee/pastries.
- Rise Biscuits in Terminal 1 is the best hot-food option but expect a 10-minute wait at peak times.
- Quietest seating is the far end of Terminal 1 near gates A1-A3.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Economy lot is cheapest long-term option ($8/day) with frequent free shuttles.
- Hourly garage closest to Terminal 2 fills by 10am on weekdays—use Daily Deck instead.
- Rideshares pick up on the lower level; allow 5 extra minutes during rain.
- Rental cars are a short walk from baggage claim in both terminals.
