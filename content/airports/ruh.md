# RUH Airport Guide

> Fast, no-fluff reference for the busiest airport in Saudi Arabia.

## Quick Facts
- Five terminals with T5 handling most Saudia international and domestic flights.
- **MCT (T3/T5 change):** no airside link — taxi/shuttle + re-clear; budget **2 h+** minimum between terminals.
- T1-T4 serve other carriers; walking between T3 and T5 takes 15-20 minutes.
- Free Wi-Fi is reliable but requires a Saudi mobile number for full speed.
- **Power:** Family rooms in T5 near gate 5 have outlets; older T1/T2 are sparse.
- **Water:** Refill fountains in T5 post-security; buy landside in T5 departures before late-night flights.
- Prayer rooms, family rooms, and women-only zones are numerous and clean.
- No airside hotel; nearest sleep options are outside the secure area.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Expect full body scans and bag searches at every terminal; liquids in 100 ml rule strictly enforced.
- Women may be directed to female-only lanes—have your passport ready.
- Avoid 20:00-23:00 and 05:00-08:00 windows; lines exceed 45 minutes.
- No TSA PreCheck equivalent; Saudia Gold and Star Gold get fast-track in T5 only.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the Riyadh Airports app before arrival for real-time gate and bus info.
- Use Terminal 5 departures hall restaurants before security if your flight is after 23:00.
- Book a driver via Careem from the arrivals level; official taxis add a 10 SAR surcharge.
- Keep a printed or offline copy of your boarding pass—Wi-Fi drops inside security.
- Family rooms in T5 near gate 5 are rarely crowded and have power outlets.
- If connecting from T3 to T5, exit and re-enter; internal transfer is not signposted.
- Carry small SAR notes for prayer room attendants and luggage cart pushers.

## Terminals & Navigation
- T5 is the newest and most comfortable; T1 and T2 feel dated and have fewer seats.
- Free inter-terminal buses run every 15 minutes but skip T5 after midnight.
- Minimum connection time is 90 minutes domestic-to-international; allow 2 hours if changing terminals.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- Saudia Alfursan Lounge in T5 is the best option for eligible passengers; food is above average.
- Al Baik in T5 departures offers consistent fried chicken at low prices.
- Quietest seating is near gates 12-18 in T5 after 22:00; avoid the central atrium.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Careem and Uber pickup is on the arrivals lower level; expect 20-40 SAR to central Riyadh.
- Official taxis charge fixed rates but add waiting fees after 10 minutes.
- Short-term parking is 5 SAR per hour; long-term lots are cheaper but require a shuttle.
