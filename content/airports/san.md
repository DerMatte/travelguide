# SAN Airport Guide

> SAN's two connected terminals and short security times make it one of the fastest major U.S. airports to clear.

## Quick Facts
- Two terminals connected by a post-security walkway; no train needed.
- Southwest dominates with most flights from Terminal 1.
- 3 miles from downtown; Uber/Lyft pickup on lower arrivals level.
- TSA PreCheck lanes at both terminals; CLEAR at Terminal 2.
- Cell phone lot on the north side offers free 30-minute wait with flight monitors.
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free **SAN-WiFi** — browser login; reliable.
- **Power:** T2 observation deck upper level and Gate A25 area — charge before Southwest banks.
- **Water:** Refill fountains post-security in both terminals — bring an empty bottle.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the SAN app or airport website for live wait times; PreCheck lines stay under 15 minutes most days.
- Avoid the main Terminal 2 checkpoint 6-9 AM and 4-7 PM; Terminal 1 checkpoint is usually faster for Southwest passengers.
- CLEAR is only at Terminal 2; pair it with PreCheck for the shortest path.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the Terminal 2 short-term garage and walk the connector if your flight is in Terminal 1; saves circling time.
- Use the north cell phone lot and watch the app; you can reach the terminal in under 4 minutes once your ride is ready.
- Grab coffee at the Terminal 1 Peet's before security if your gate is in T2; lines are shorter and you can carry it through.
- For early flights, check bags at the curb even without a boarding pass; agents open 2 hours early and move faster than inside counters.
- Download the free SAN app for gate alerts; it pushes changes 5-10 minutes before airline apps.
- If connecting from Alaska to Southwest, exit security and re-enter at the faster checkpoint rather than using the long walkway.
- The Terminal 2 observation deck on the upper level gives a quiet spot with charging outlets and flight views.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Terminal 1 handles Southwest and some international departures; Terminal 2 holds everyone else.
- The airside walkway between T1 and T2 takes 7-9 minutes at normal pace; budget 12 minutes with bags.
- Gates in T1 run 1-11; T2 gates run 20-51. Common mistake is assuming all gates are close after security.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Club at SAN in Terminal 2 near gate 30 offers day passes and solid Wi-Fi; best quiet option.
- Stone Brewing in T2 provides reliable local food and beer; other solid choices include Craft Brews and the San Diego Fish Co.
- Terminal 1 has fewer standout options; expect basic grab-and-go after security.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Rideshares pick up on the arrivals level; expect $18-25 to downtown and 10-20 minutes.
- Short-term parking in Terminal 2 garage costs $3 for the first 30 minutes; long-term lots are $18/day with free shuttle.
- Rental cars are on-site; return early if your flight is before 8 AM to avoid shuttle delays.
