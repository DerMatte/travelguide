# SIN Airport Guide

> Changi rewards early arrival with free entertainment and fast processes — use the time instead of rushing through.

## Quick Facts
- **Layout:** **T1–T3** form the main complex (connected airside via Skytrain and Jewel); **T4** sits ~1 km away and requires Skytrain or shuttle bus from **T2**.
- **Dominant carrier:** **Singapore Airlines** and subsidiaries operate most long-haul from **T2** and **T3**; **Scoot** and **Jetstar** use **T4**.
- **Attractions (airside):** **Butterfly Garden (T3 only)**; **Cactus Garden (T1)**, **Sunflower Garden (T2)**; **free 24-hour movie theatres in T2 and T3** — not in every terminal.
- **Jewel Changi:** Landside between **T1–T3** — **HSBC Rain Vortex**, dining, and **Jewel Early Check-in Lounge** (08:00–23:59).
- **MCT (same ticket):** **60 min** domestic–domestic or Schengen-style connections can work; **90 min+** for int'l–int'l or **T4** transfers; immigration adds time for arrivals entering Singapore.
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free airport Wi‑Fi — registration required; sessions may time out.
- **Liquids:** Standard **100 ml** rule in a clear bag; CT lanes may allow devices to stay in the bag — follow lane signage.

## Security & Screening Tips
- **Automated clearance:** Most nationalities can use automated gates — have passport and boarding pass ready.
- **Peak windows:** **07:00–09:00** and **19:00–21:00** when regional departures peak — queues can reach **20 min**.
- **No TSA PreCheck equivalent:** Liquids in **100 ml** containers still required in clear bags.
- **Families / elderly:** Priority lanes at **T1–T3** when staffed — ask at entry if eligible.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Butterfly Garden (T3 only):** World's first airport butterfly garden — open **24/7**, free, in the **T3 transit area**. From other terminals, ride the **Skytrain** to T3 (e.g. from **T1** near Gate D40, from **T2** via transit area E).
- **Free cinemas (T2 & T3):** 24-hour movie theatres airside — comfortable seats for layovers **3+ h**; not available in **T1** or **T4**.
- **Jewel Rain Vortex:** Viewing deck on **Level 5** landside — best before security or on a long landside layover.
- **Early check-in:** **Jewel Early Check-in Lounge** (08:00–23:59) and participating airline desks allow bag drop **3–24 h** before departure depending on carrier — verify with your airline. You can only enter the transit area **24 h** before departure due to immigration rules.
- **T4 connections:** Budget extra time — shuttle to **T2** for Skytrain/MRT; fewer lounges and dining options than T1–T3.
- **Foot reflexology:** Free stations in **T2** and **T3** airside — no appointment needed.
- **Avoid:** Assuming every terminal has the same attractions; trekking to **T3 Butterfly Garden** with under **60 min** to board from **T4**.

## Terminals & Navigation
- **T1–T3:** Connected airside via **Skytrain** and walks through **Jewel** (landside). Skytrain ride **3–5 min** between most pairs.
- **T4:** Isolated — **Skytrain from T2** or shuttle bus; no direct airside link to T1/T3 without landside transfer.
- **Walking T1↔T3 via Jewel:** **10–15 min** landside; Skytrain is faster in rain.
- **Common mistake:** Assuming **T4** has the same amenities or MRT access — take the **free shuttle to T2** for the **East-West Line**.

## Lounges Food & Amenities
- **Singapore Airlines lounges (T2/T3):** Best for eligible **Star Alliance** premium/status passengers — showers and buffet quality vary by lounge.
- **Paid options:** **The Haven (T2)** and **SATS Premier (T3)** offer showers and à-la-carte dining — verify day-pass rules before travel.
- **Food:** **Singapore Food Street (T2)** and **Jewel food hall** beat most chain outlets; **T4** has fewer standout options.
- **Luggage wrapping:** **Paid** service at **Smarte Carte** / **Changi Recommends** counters — not free.
- **Quiet spots:** **YOTELAIR** day rooms (T1, paid) or **T3 cinema recliners** (free) for short rest.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **MRT (cheapest):** **East-West Line** from **T2/T3 basement** — from **~S$2.50** to city; runs roughly **05:30–23:00** (check SMRT for exact last train).
- **T4 → MRT:** Free shuttle bus to **T2**, then MRT.
- **Taxi:** Managed queue at **T1–T3 basement**; airport surcharge applies after **22:00**.
- **Parking:** **T3** short-stay is convenient for quick drop-offs; rates rise sharply after **2 h**.
