# SIN Airport Guide

> Changi rewards early arrival with free entertainment and fast processes — use the time instead of rushing through.

## Quick Facts
- **Terminals:** **T1, T2, T3** form the main complex (connected airside via Skytrain); **T4** sits apart (~**1 km**) and serves mainly **Scoot, Jetstar**, and other LCCs.
- **Singapore Airlines** and subsidiaries operate most long-haul from **T2 and T3**.
- **Jewel Changi:** Landside between **T1–T3** — **HSBC Rain Vortex**, shopping, dining; reachable without boarding pass from T1/T2/T3 arrival areas.
- **Signature attractions (transit/airside):** **Butterfly Garden** and the **free 24-hour cinema** are both in **Terminal 3 only** — use Skytrain if you're in another terminal.
- **MRT (Changi Airport station):** Basement of **T2/T3** area — **~45 min** to city, **S$2–3**; last trains around **23:00** (check SMRT for exact times).
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free unlimited airport Wi‑Fi with simple registration.

## Security & Screening Tips
- **Automated clearance:** Passport + boarding pass for biometric lanes at most checkpoints; have documents ready.
- **Peak queues:** **07:00–09:00** and **19:00–21:00** — can reach **~20 min**; off-peak is often faster.
- **Liquids:** Standard **100 ml** in clear bag — CT lanes may allow devices to stay in bag at equipped checkpoints.
- **Families / elderly:** Priority lanes at **T1–T3** — ask staff at checkpoint entry.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- **Butterfly Garden (T3 transit, Level 2):** World's first airport butterfly garden — **1,000+ butterflies**, open **24/7** ([Changi](https://www.changiairport.com/en/experience/attractions-directory/butterfly-garden.html)). From **T1/T2**, Skytrain to **T3** (~**5 min**); allow **20+ min** round trip from distant gates.
- **Free cinema (T3 transit, Level 3):** ~**50 seats**, **24/7** screenings — closed **Thu 11:00–16:00** for maintenance ([movie theatre](https://www.changiairport.com/en/at-changi/facilities-and-services-directory/movie-theatres.html)).
- **Jewel before security:** If you have **2+ h** landside, Rain Vortex and food hall beat generic gate seating — you must re-clear immigration/security if entering transit afterward.
- **T4 connections:** Budget extra time — Skytrain/bus to **T1/T2/T3**, fewer lounges, no direct MRT at T4.
- **Download the Changi app** for Skytrain times and gate changes.

## Terminals & Navigation
- **T1 ↔ T2 ↔ T3:** Skytrain **~3–5 min**; walking via Jewel landside **10–15 min** (requires exiting transit if airside).
- **T4:** Shuttle bus or Skytrain connection to main complex — plan **15–20 min** for inter-terminal moves.
- **Common mistake:** Assuming every terminal has the same amenities — **butterfly garden, cinema, and best gardens** are **T3-centric**.

## Lounges Food & Amenities
- **KLM/SIA-quality lounges:** **SilverKris** (SIA) in **T2/T3**; paid options include **Ambassador Transit Lounge** and **SATS Premier**.
- **Food:** **Singapore Food Street (T2)**, **Jewel food hall (landside)**, hawker-style options beat most chains.
- **Sleep:** Paid **YOTELAIR** pods in **T1/T4**; free cinema recliners in **T3** for short naps.
- **Water:** Refill stations in newer transit areas — bring an empty bottle.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- **MRT:** Cheapest to city (**S$2–3**); **T2/T3** basement access.
- **Taxi:** Managed queues at **T1–T3** basement; airport surcharge applies.
- **Parking:** Short-stay at **T3** most convenient; rates rise sharply after **2 h**.
