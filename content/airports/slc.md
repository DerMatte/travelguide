# SLC Airport Guide

> Fast, low-stress navigation for Delta hub travelers who want to minimize time on the ground.

## Quick Facts
- Delta Air Lines primary hub with ~300 daily departures.
- Single terminal split into Concourses A and B connected by a 5-minute indoor walkway.
- TSA PreCheck and CLEAR lanes keep average security wait under 15 minutes.
- Free Wi-Fi, cell phone lot with live camera feeds, and strong on-time stats.
- Serves 25+ million passengers annually; compact layout reduces walking.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the east checkpoint for PreCheck/CLEAR during 6-9 a.m. Delta banks; it moves faster than the west.
- CLEAR members can bypass the ID check line entirely and head straight to biometric screening.
- Avoid the 4:30-6:30 p.m. window on Sunday evenings when regional flights cluster.
- Liquids in a clear quart bag still required; no known water refill quirks.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Park in the cell phone lot and watch the app for your flight's "arrived at gate" push before driving to arrivals.
- Download the SLC app for gate-change alerts that beat airline notifications by several minutes.
- If connecting on Delta with under 40 minutes, stay airside and use the A-B walkway instead of the shuttle.
- Squatters Pub in the main terminal offers faster service and better beer than most post-security spots.
- Charge devices at the quiet seating area near Gate A25; outlets are plentiful and rarely crowded.
- Skip the rental-car shuttle wait by using the on-site economy lot when returning a car.
- Pre-order coffee via the airport app at the Starbucks near Baggage Claim for a 2-minute pickup.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Everything is under one roof; Concourse A handles most Delta flights, B the rest.
- The indoor connector between A and B takes 5 minutes at normal pace; no need to exit security.
- Walking time from security to farthest gate is about 12 minutes; moving walkways cover half the distance.
- Common mistake: exiting to the shuttle when the walkway is faster and weather-protected.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- Delta Sky Club in Concourse A is the only paid lounge; day passes available if flying Delta.
- Squatters Pub (pre-security) serves solid local beer and burgers with short lines.
- Post-security, the best quick option is the Great Hall food hall near the connector for variety.
- Quiet seating with outlets clusters near Gate A25 and the end of Concourse B.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Economy lot is cheapest on-site option at ~$12/day; free shuttle runs every 10 minutes.
- Rideshares pick up on the lower level; expect 5-10 minute wait during peak arrival banks.
- TRAX light rail connects directly to downtown in 20 minutes for $2.50; runs every 15 minutes.
- Cell phone lot has live camera views via the SLC app so you can time arrivals perfectly.
