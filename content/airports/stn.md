# STN Airport Guide

> Stansted rewards travelers who treat it like a Ryanair bus station: arrive early, stay landside, and move fast.

## Quick Facts
- One terminal, two security halls; Ryanair dominates every gate.
- Stansted Express and National Express coaches both leave from the same forecourt.
- Free 24-hour shuttle runs between terminal and long-stay car parks.
- No train service after 23:00; last coach departs around 01:00.
- Passport control uses e-gates for most EU/UK/Irish passengers.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the airport app to pick the shorter of the two security halls.
- Clear liquids in 100 ml containers only; no PreCheck equivalent exists.
- Avoid 06:00-08:30 and 18:00-21:00 if possible; those windows regularly exceed 40-minute waits.
- Families and passengers with reduced mobility get a dedicated lane—ask staff at the entrance.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Print your Ryanair boarding pass at the self-service kiosks outside security if your phone dies.
- The WHSmith landside sells cheap meal-deal sandwiches that beat most airside prices.
- Drop bags at the far-left bag-drop zone; it usually moves faster than the central desks.
- For the Stansted Express, buy tickets on the platform machine rather than the app during peak hours to skip the queue.
- Long-stay parking is cheaper if you pre-book online and enter the terminal via the free shuttle.
- Need a quiet corner? The mezzanine level above check-in has seating and power outlets with almost no foot traffic.
- Download the airport app offline map; mobile signal drops in the baggage claim area.

## Terminals & Navigation
- Everything happens in one building; follow purple signs for departures and green for arrivals.
- Walking time from security to farthest gate is 12-15 minutes; allow extra if you need to clear immigration first.
- No internal train or people mover—flat walking only.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Escape Lounge landside offers better value than the two airside options and includes hot food.
- For actual meals, the landside food court (Pret, Itsu, Tortilla) beats the overpriced airside branches.
- Quietest seating is on the upper level near the observation deck, open 24 hours.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Stansted Express is fastest to central London; buy return tickets for the lowest per-trip cost.
- National Express coaches reach more London stops and are cheaper if booked ahead.
- On-site short-stay parking costs £6.50 for 30 minutes; long-stay is £9-12 per day when pre-booked.
- Last train leaves at 23:00; after that the only option is a coach or a £120+ taxi.
