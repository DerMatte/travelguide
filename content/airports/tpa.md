# TPA Airport Guide

> TPA rewards travelers who know the monorail layout and skip the morning rush.

## Quick Facts
- Single main terminal feeds four airsides via elevated monorail.
- Southwest dominates with gates in Airside A; Delta and American split C/E/F.
- Standard security rarely exceeds 20 minutes; PreCheck and CLEAR lanes move even faster.
- On-site parking garage connects directly to the terminal via short walk or moving walkways.
- Tampa Bay area traffic is light outside weekday rush hours.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Use the main terminal checkpoint; it is the only one and splits into PreCheck/CLEAR on the left.
- Avoid 6-8am Monday and Friday when local business travelers peak.
- CLEAR members still need to reach the checkpoint early if traveling with non-CLEAR companions.
- TSA PreCheck enrollment is processed on-site if you qualify for renewal.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the official TPA app before arrival for live monorail and checkpoint times.
- If your flight is in Airside E or F, park on the west side of the garage for the shortest walk.
- Grab a Cuban sandwich at Columbia in Airside C instead of fighting the Starbucks line.
- Use the quiet seating area near the windows in the main terminal between security and the monorail.
- When connecting from Southwest to Delta, ride the monorail one stop early and walk the short bridge to avoid crowds.
- Free Wi-Fi is fast enough for video calls; no login required.
- Check the departure boards in the main terminal before riding the monorail—gates sometimes flip to a different airside.

## Terminals & Navigation
- All passengers enter the main terminal, clear security, then take the monorail to their airside.
- Monorail ride between any two airsides takes 3-5 minutes; trains run every 2-3 minutes.
- Walking between airsides is possible but long and poorly signed—avoid it.
- Baggage claim and ground transport are on the lower level of the main terminal.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The Delta Sky Club in Airside E is the only major lounge; access is limited outside Delta premium cabins.
- Columbia in Airside C and Cigar City in Airside F are the standout local options.
- Airside A has a solid Shake Shack and quieter seating near the far end.
- Nursing rooms and family restrooms are located in the main terminal near each monorail entrance.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- Short-term garage is $3 for the first hour, $24 daily; long-term economy is $12 daily with free shuttle.
- Uber/Lyft pickup is on the lower level; follow signs to avoid the taxi stand.
- Rental cars are in a separate garage reached by free shuttle from the main terminal.
- Weekday morning drop-offs are fastest using the cell-phone lot then driving straight to departures.
