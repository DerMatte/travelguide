# TPE Airport Guide

> TPE moves fast once you know the terminal layout and peak immigration windows.

## Quick Facts
- Two terminals linked by free shuttle buses running every 5-10 minutes.
- **MCT (same ticket, T1↔T2):** shuttle **8–12 min** — budget **75 min** minimum including immigration peaks; **90 min** safer.
- EVA Air, Starlux, and China Airlines dominate; most U.S. flights depart from T2.
- Direct MRT to Taipei Main Station costs NT$160 and takes 35-40 minutes.
- Free, fast Wi-Fi available everywhere; no login required.
- **Power:** 4th-floor observation deck in T1; gate C6 shower area in T2 — charge early.
- **Water:** Free drinking water stations near every gate cluster — bring an empty bottle.
- Currency exchange desks close at 11 PM; ATMs remain open 24/7.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Expect 60-90 minutes for immigration + security during 7-10 AM and 6-9 PM peaks.
- Remove laptops and liquids only after the initial document check.
- Families and elderly travelers can use the priority lane at T2 immigration.
- No TSA PreCheck equivalent; arrive early rather than relying on shortcuts.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Download the Taoyuan Airport app for live immigration wait times before exiting the MRT.
- Landside beef noodle stands in T2 (near check-in row D) beat all airside options.
- Use the free shower rooms in T2 near gate C6 if you have a long layover.
- Book the Taoyuan Airport MRT last train (11:52 PM from T1) only if your flight lands before 10 PM.
- T1 has quieter seating near the observation deck on the 4th floor when T2 is packed.
- Grab a portable charger at the 7-Eleven in T2 arrivals before heading to the gate.
- If connecting to a domestic flight, exit and re-enter rather than using the slow transfer bus.

## Terminals & Navigation
- T1 handles most legacy carriers; T2 is newer and used by EVA, Starlux, and many international flights.
- Walking time between farthest gates in T2 is 12-15 minutes; T1 is slightly smaller.
- Free shuttle stops are clearly marked outside both terminals; do not walk between them.
- Common mistake: assuming all EVA flights are in T2—double-check your boarding pass.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- The best paid lounge is the Plaza Premium in T2 (near gate C3); avoid the crowded T1 version.
- Landside food in T2 (rows C and D) offers superior Taiwanese options at half the airside price.
- Quietest seating is the 4th-floor observation deck in T1 or the far end of T2 near gate D10.
- Free drinking water stations are located near every gate cluster.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- MRT is fastest and cheapest to central Taipei; taxis cost NT$1,200-1,500 with possible surcharges at night.
- Airport bus 1819 to Taipei Main Station runs every 15 minutes and takes 50-60 minutes.
- Short-term parking (first 30 minutes free) is only useful for pickups; long-term lots fill by 8 AM.
- Book a taxi via the official app to skip the queue during evening rush.
