# WAW Airport Guide

> Fast, no-nonsense reference for moving through Warsaw Chopin without wasted time.

## Quick Facts
- Single terminal with Piers A (Schengen) and B (non-Schengen)
- **MCT (same ticket):** **45 min** Schengen–Schengen; **60 min** A↔B with passport control and possible re-screen.
- LOT Polish Airlines hub with Star Alliance priority
- SKM rail reaches city center in 20 min for 4.40 PLN
- All non-Schengen flights require passport control after security
- Security opens 2.5 hours before first flights; closes at last departure
- **Wi‑Fi:** Free airport Wi‑Fi — reliable; use official app for gate alerts.
- **Power:** Benches near gate A1 have outlets; elsewhere sparse.
- **Water:** Refill fountains post-security in Pier A — bring an empty bottle.

## Security & Screening Tips
- Remove liquids and electronics only at the X-ray; trays are provided.
- Fast-track lanes (paid, ~25 PLN) sit left of standard queues and rarely exceed 10 min.
- Avoid 6:00-8:30 and 17:30-20:00 windows; arrive outside those blocks.
- Non-Schengen passengers face a second passport check after security—budget 15 min.

## Best Airport Tricks & Hacks
- Buy SKM train tickets from machines on level -1 before exiting arrivals.
- Landside food court on the departures level has better Polish food than airside.
- Free charging lockers are behind the information desk near check-in row 3.
- If your flight is delayed, exit security and re-enter; re-entry is allowed until 30 min before departure.
- Priority security is included with LOT Business or Star Alliance Gold—show card at fast-track entrance.
- Download the Chopin Airport app for gate push notifications; SMS alerts lag.
- Currency exchange machines airside give worse rates—exchange landside or use ATMs.

## Terminals & Navigation
- One building only; Piers A and B are connected post-security by a short walkway.
- Schengen to non-Schengen connections require full re-screening—allow 45 min minimum.
- Walking time from check-in to farthest gate is under 12 min.

## Lounges, Food & Amenities
- LOT Business Lounge (airside, Pier A) is the only worthwhile paid option; day pass ~120 PLN.
- Pre-security: Bar Mleczny milk bar and Zapiecek pierogi stand offer solid cheap meals.
- Quiet seating: rows of benches near gate A1 with power outlets and fewer announcements.

## Ground Transport & Parking
- SKM rail is fastest and cheapest to city; trains every 10-15 min.
- Taxi apps (Free Now, Bolt) queue at arrivals level -1; fixed airport tariff is rarely enforced.
- Short-term parking P1 costs 8 PLN for first 30 min, then 6 PLN per hour—use only for pick-up.
